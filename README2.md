Typing Speed Test Game 

A simple console typing speed test. Type random words as fast and accurately as possible.

Features:
- Random words from a list
- Tracks correct and wrong attempts
- Measures time and calculates WPM

How to Run:
1. Compile: gcc game2.c -o game2
2. Run: ./game2(Linux/Mac) or game2.exe (Windows)

Controls:
- Type the displayed word and press Enter
